ABOUT ME



I hold a Bachelor's degree and pursued further studies in ISIMS . My passion for game development led me to delve into this field during the summer, resulting in the successful release of two games. Eager to broaden my knowledge, I enrolled in a Big Data program at my faculty.
I actively contribute to the tech community as a member of the Google Students Club at ISIMS, where I specialize as a game developer. As part of this community, I've organized three events with the Google Developer Students Club and Google Developer Group, fostering collaboration and innovation.
In addition to my programming skills, I explored 3D modeling, creating a detailed model of my faculty. This newfound expertise allowed me to contribute to a startup as a part-time 3D modeler. In this role, I've also crafted numerous 3D animations for event marketing, effectively communicating ideas and promoting engagement.
Notably, I took on the challenge of developing a tournament game, showcasing my ability to create interactive and engaging experiences. My dedication to both technical and creative aspects of the industry has allowed me to thrive in diverse roles, from game development to 3D modeling and animations. I am excited about the endless possibilities in the world of technology and eager to continue contributing to its advancement.
