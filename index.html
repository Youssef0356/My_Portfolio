ABOUT ME



I am a third-year Bachelor’s student at ISIMS, passionate about technology and innovation. My journey into game development began during a summer break, resulting in the release of two successful games. To broaden my knowledge, I enrolled in a Big Data program at my faculty.
As an active member of the tech community, I contributed to the Google Students Club at ISIMS as a game developer and later led the game development team. I organized three events with the Google Developer Students Club and Google Developer Group, fostering collaboration and innovation.
I work as a freelance 3D modeler and animator, assisting students with their end-of-year projects. My skills include web development (React, Tailwind for frontend, .NET for backend), video editing, augmented reality development, and image editing. I have also created a detailed 3D model of my faculty, leading to a part-time role at a startup where I crafted 3D animations for event marketing.
One notable project was developing a tournament game, demonstrating my ability to create interactive and engaging experiences. My dedication to both technical and creative aspects has allowed me to excel in various roles, from game development to 3D modeling and animation. I am excited about the endless possibilities in technology and eager to continue contributing to its advancement.
