ABOUT ME


I am a third-year Bachelor’s student at ISIMS, pursuing a degree in technology and innovation. Alongside my studies, I work as a freelance 3D modeler and animator, collaborating with a diverse range of clients, including companies, educators, and students. My expertise encompasses 3D animation, video editing, augmented reality development, and image editing. I have a proven track record of creating impactful 3D animations for event marketing and providing high-quality video editing services.
Currently, I am a member of the Virtual Exhibition Space for Technical Historical Objects, collaborating with institutions such as ISIMS, HKA Germany, University Malaysia Pahang, and GUTECH. This project allows me to contribute to the preservation and presentation of historical artifacts through innovative digital solutions.
I am dedicated to delivering engaging content that helps clients bring their ideas to life through cutting-edge design and animation techniques. My focus on quality and creativity drives my work, allowing me to excel in various aspects of visual storytelling and design.
